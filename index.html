What these numbers represent

The amounts above show an estimated proportional share of your property tax payment across major city service categories. Results are shown in whole dollars.

Small rounding differences are handled by adjusting the final category so totals always match the amount entered.

What’s included in each category

Public Safety

Police, fire, emergency response, inspections, and related services.

Public Works

Streets, sidewalks, stormwater systems, snow removal, and infrastructure.

Health & Social Services

Public and environmental health functions and limited social services.

Culture & Recreation

Parks, recreation programs, library services, and cultural amenities.

Community & Economic Development

Planning, housing‑related activities, and economic development efforts.

General Government

Governance, finance, legal services, elections, and administration.

Debt Service

Principal and interest payments on existing city debt.

Capital Projects

Construction, major repairs, and long‑term investments in public facilities.

Categories group many services together and are not exhaustive.
