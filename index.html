How to read this

City revenues (property taxes, fees, state and federal funds) are pooled and governed by law. Individual tax dollars are not tracked to specific services.

This tool uses your property tax payment as a reference amount and applies the same proportions the City uses to report total Governmental Activities spending.

Monthly figures are shown to make costs easier to understand. They represent average service costs, not bills or direct charges.
