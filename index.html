How to read this

City revenues (including property taxes, fees, and state or federal funds) are pooled and governed by legal restrictions. Because of this, individual tax dollars are not tracked to specific services.

This tool uses your property tax payment as a reference amount and applies the same proportions the City uses to report total Governmental Activities spending. It shows how your contribution compares to the scale of services provided citywide.

Results are shown in whole dollars. Small rounding differences are handled by adjusting the final category so totals always match the amount entered.
