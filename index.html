How to read this receipt

When you pay property taxes, your payment is distributed among several local governments, including schools, the County, and the City.

This receipt focuses only on the City. It estimates how much of a typical property tax payment supports City operations and shows how those City resources are spread across major City‑provided services.

The City’s property tax levy for FY26 totals approximately $15.0 million, supporting core City services such as police, fire, streets, administration, and capital improvements.

This tool does not track individual dollars. It provides a descriptive, City‑only estimate based on adopted levy totals and City service costs.
