About This Site

This site was created by Layne Pieri, a resident of Marshalltown.

I am not affiliated with the City of Marshalltown or any other local government in any official capacity for this project.

The goal of this site is to reduce the cognitive barrier to understanding how City property tax funding works: what the City actually receives, what it is legally responsible for, and how City services are funded in practice.

All calculations and explanations are based on publicly available documents. The tool is intentionally descriptive rather than prescriptive; it does not advocate for specific policies, tax levels, or budget decisions.
