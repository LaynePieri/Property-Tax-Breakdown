Sources & Methodology

This page documents the public sources and calculation approach used to build the City services receipt.

Primary sources

  • City of Marshalltown FY26 adopted budget
  • City Council proceedings – April 28, 2025
  • Marshall County certified property tax rates (Taxes Payable FY2025–2026)

Methodology (plain language)

  1. County tax‑rate tables are used to estimate the portion of a total property tax bill attributable to the City, based on school district.
  2. City FY26 governmental expenditures are grouped into standard functional categories and converted into percentage shares.
  3. Those percentages are applied to the estimated City portion of the tax payment entered by the user.

Results are shown in whole dollars. Minor rounding differences are resolved so totals remain consistent.
